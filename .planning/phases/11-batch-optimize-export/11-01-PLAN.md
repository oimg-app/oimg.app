<objective>
Refactor `runOptimize` so per-file write-back happens AS each worker returns, not after `Promise.allSettled` settles the whole batch. Implements D-03 (live per-row status during batch) and D-11 (skip `done` files).

Purpose: Today the FileRow status dots stay `queued` for the entire batch then all flip at once when allSettled resolves, defeating OPT-02 SC-1 ("live per-file progress"). This is the single highest-leverage change in Phase 11 — every other UI affordance (aggregate counter, indeterminate encoding spinner, disable-then-explain) depends on the status field reflecting reality during the batch.

Output: A streaming `runOptimize` that preserves the bounded WorkerPool's concurrency cap (Pitfall 1 in RESEARCH.md), and an e2e test that asserts per-row status transitions are observable mid-batch.
</objective>
